### âœ¨ Step 3: Choosing a Sign-Up Method on Try Hack Me

On the registration page, Try Hack Me offers two secure sign-up methods: **Google Sign-Up** and **Email Sign-Up**.

*   **Google Sign-Up:** For most beginners, this process is the fastest and simplest. You only need to choose your Google account, review the permissions, and click allow. This method avoids manual password creation.
*   **Email Sign-Up:** A valid email address must be entered along with a strong password (minimum of eight characters recommended). This may require email verification to activate the account.

Both methods are safe, and users should select the one that best fits their security preferences.
